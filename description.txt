War Machine Invasion
This is a small addon for the Calamity Mod, designed to expand pre-Hardmode gameplay and delve deeper into the lore of the Wulfrum machines.

Current content:
1 new Boss
3 new NPCs
4 new Weapons
10+ new Items

Boss Battle Theme:

We plan to add more weapons, items, and accessories in future updates.
